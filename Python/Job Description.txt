A day in the life
As an Amazon software engineer in this team you will design, develop, test and deploy world-class
bespoke financial software to support our expanding global video business.
You will build systems responsible for delivering the latest video content to customers, onboarding
major global studios and their contracts worth billions of dollars. As we deal with financial data,
budget management, accounting and payments the code you write must deal with accuracy and
consistency, while still operating at scale.
You will build and design software using modern AWS technology (Lambda, DynamoDB, CDK, etc.),
Java, and full-stack technologies (React, Nodejs).
About the hiring group
Financial Systems mission is to ensure timeliness and accuracy of Prime Video’s financial processes.
We believe this is best achieved by automating those process from end to end, removing human
intervention, allowing our business to scale, delivering great content to our customers fast! We are
responsible for royalty onboarding, payment planning, royalty calculation and payment, royalty
accounting, and device bounty payments.
Our team believes in transparent communication, open discussion on design ideas, and have a failfast,
Agile, mindset. We expect everyone to engage in discussions and want to continue developing
an innovative culture where every idea is listened to. The team owns the end-to-end delivery
including supporting our customers.
